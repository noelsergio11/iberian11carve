Intro

Iberian Carve specializes in providing an exclusive Ham Carving service for weddings and important events for you and your family, enhancing luxury and aiming to create an unforgettable sensory experience for our clients. Iberian Carve is more than a service -it's a story of craftsmanship, passion, and the pursuit of perfection.

Our founder, a true connoisseur of Spanish gastronomy, recognized that the art of Ham Carving is more than just slicing - it is an experience, a performance that brings people together. Inspired by the elegance and precision of traditional Spanish cortadores, Iberian Carve was born to offer an exclusive service because you deserve it. Your wedding day is one of the most important moments of your life, and we are dedicated to making it extraordinary. Imagine an expert cortador, dressed in classic attire, meticulously carving thin, delicate slices of rich, flavorful JAMON IBERICO DE BELLOTA before your guests.

At Iberian Carve, we focus on blending tradition with modern sophistication. Our goal is to create a unique and memorable experience for your special day, ensuring that every detail is taken care of with the utmost attention. From the small details to the grand moments, we are going to make your event a truly exceptional celebration.
